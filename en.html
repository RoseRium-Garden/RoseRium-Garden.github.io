Frequently asked questions FAQ

What is the XP plugin for?

In order for you to be able to increase your rate on the server (in other words, the extraction of resources from certain objects increases) it is also needed in order to buy items for constant experience, which is displayed in the plugin when entering the /XP command in the game chat

When does a server wipe happen?

The wipe occurs every first and third Thursday of the month and also, if the month is 5 weeks long, the wipe occurs on the 5th week of the month on Thursday

What is the initial rate on the server?

Classic rate X1

When does the server restart?

Every day at 2:00 naght Moscow time

How to level up XP?

any actions that are aimed at mining or quarrying, barrels, stones, etc.
